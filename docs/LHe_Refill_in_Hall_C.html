Liquid Helium refill memorandum

Posted 2021/09/09

  1. Set the level reader mode to "10 s” on the software
    • Set the level mode in “fast” mode
  2. Liquid Helium can preparation
    • Connect the Helium gas tube from He-1 bottle to the can
      • (Remember to clean the entire connector)
    • Insert the liquid Helium transfer line (long one or short one)
  3. Open V3 valve (the red one on liquefier panel)
    • (It is the compressor bypass)
  4. Open Main Bath to the CRESST balloon
    • (Red valve to balloon in pumping room + black valve behind the main panel)
  5. Plug the “BOA” into the liquid Helium transfer line on the top of the can
  6. Reduce the liquefier inlet flow down to 3.6 ~ 3.7
  7. Start the liquid Helium refill
    1. Keep 20 ~ 30 mbar over-pressure in the liquid Helium can
    2. Open the transfer line needle valve with 1/2 turn
    3. Wait for the flame
    4. Insert the “BOA” in the liquid Helium transfer line on the side of cryostat
      • You will see the pressure of Main Bath going up
      • (The best is just below the black sign)
    5. Start chronometer
      • After 12 ~ 15 min, you will see the pressure of Main Bath going down in the range 1000 ~ 950 mbar
      • (This means you are transferring liquid Helium)
    6. Open completely the transfer line needle valve
    7. Keep the over-pressure in the can in the range 100 c~ 150 mbar
  8. Stop the liquid Helium refill when reach at ~82% and wait 5 min more
  9. Close safety valve on the can
  10. Close Main Bath to the CRESST balloon
    • (Red valve to balloon in pumping room + black valve behind the main panel)
    • Make sure that the pressure of Main Bath stay similar
  11. Restore “900 s” reading and “slow” mode on level meter
  12. Start to progressively close V3
    • Keep the flow meter ball below 35
  13. When V3 is completely closed, wait Main Bath evaporation flow recover below ~14
  14. Start increasing the liquefier inlet until ~9.00
  15. Remove the “BOA” and the Helium transfer line from the can
  16. Remove the Helium gas line from the can and remember to close the He-1 bottle
